HiDPI

Retina on your yucky non-retina Mac!*

*Kind of

As described in the picture above, HiDPI is a hidden preference on your Mac that allows you to use something like Retina on an ordinary boring old normal computer.

So when you use it it just looks like everything's scaled bigger but a higher resolution, right? That's exactly what it's doing!

It's rendering everything with four times more detail, just like Retina, but because you have a really bad non-Retina display, it needs to render four times bigger to show that detail.

So it's a trade-off, but still fun to mess around with :)
